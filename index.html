About Freedom Tower

Freedom Tower is a decentralized initiative dedicated to reclaiming personal sovereignty through open-source tools, transparent knowledge, and resilient digital infrastructure. We are not a company. We are not a brand. We are not a product. We are a movement.

The 7 Pillars of Freedom Tower

  • Own your data
  • Host your own systems
  • Share knowledge freely and openly
  • Build redundancies into your life
  • Respect privacy and freedom
  • Collaborate, don't dominate
  • Empower future generations
